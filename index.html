How To Play

Quick guide for each game on this site.

♟️ Chess

  1. Each piece moves in a specific way (pawns forward, bishops diagonal, etc.).
  2. The goal is to checkmate the opponent's King.
  3. Play vs. the computer or pass the keyboard to a friend.

🔢 Sudoku

  1. The board has a 9×9 grid divided into nine 3×3 boxes.
  2. Fill every empty cell with a number from 1 to 9.
  3. Each number can appear only once per row, column, and box.

🔴 Checkers

  1. Move your pieces diagonally on dark squares only.
  2. Jump over an opponent's piece to capture it.
  3. Reach the far end to become a King — Kings can move backwards too.
  4. Capture all opponent pieces to win.

🃏 Solitaire

  1. Move cards between columns — lower cards go on higher ones of a different colour.
  2. Uncover all face-down cards.
  3. Stack all four suits (Ace to King) in the top foundation piles to win.

⚫ Reversi

  1. Place a disc to trap at least one opponent disc between yours.
  2. All trapped discs flip to your colour.
  3. The player with the most discs when the board is full wins.

🔤 Word Puzzle

  1. Look at the letter grid and find all the hidden words from the list.
  2. Words can go horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  3. Click the first letter then the last letter of a word to mark it.

🟦 Falling Cubes

  1. Blocks fall from the top of the screen.
  2. Use arrow keys to move left/right and rotate them.
  3. Fill a complete horizontal line to clear it and earn points.
  4. Game ends when blocks stack up to the top.
